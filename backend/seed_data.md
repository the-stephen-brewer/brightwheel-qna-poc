# Sunshine Early Learning - Core Policies

## Tuition Policy
Infant tuition is $375 per week. Toddler tuition is $325 per week. Preschool tuition is $275 per week. Tuition is due every Monday by 9:00 AM. A late fee of $25 is applied if payment is not received by Tuesday at 6:00 PM. Tuition includes all snacks and meals, but does not include formula or diapers for infants.

## Fever and Illness Policy
Children must be kept home if they have a fever of 100.4°F or higher. They must be fever-free without the use of fever-reducing medication for at least 24 hours before returning to the center. If a child develops a fever while at the center, the parent will be notified and must pick up the child within one hour. For outbreaks of contagious diseases (like hand-foot-and-mouth or COVID-19), the center follows CDC guidelines and may require a doctor's note for return.

## Holiday Schedule
Sunshine Early Learning is closed on the following holidays: New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day and the Friday after, and Christmas Day. We are also closed for professional development on the first Monday of October. We remain open on Veterans Day and Presidents' Day.

## Lunch and Nutrition
We provide a healthy lunch and two snacks (morning and afternoon) daily. Our menu follows USDA nutritional guidelines and is posted weekly on the parent board. If your child has specific dietary restrictions or allergies, please provide a signed medical statement. For children who prefer to bring lunch from home, please ensure it is nut-free as we are a nut-free facility.
